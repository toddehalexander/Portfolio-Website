This project is a Pokemon battle game where players can compete against an AI opponent. The game simulates the core mechanics of the Pokemon franchise, including choosing a team of Pokemon, battling with moves and abilities, and determining a winner based on the remaining Pokemon's health.

  • Pokemon Team Selection: Players can assemble a team of Pokemon to battle with.

  • Web-based Frontend: The user interface is built using React, allowing for an interactive and visually appealing experience.

ğŸ® How to Play

  1. Visit the game's website and select your team of Pokemon.
  2. The game will begin, and you will be prompted to choose your first Pokemon.
  3. Each turn, you can select a move to use against the AI's Pokemon or switch to a different Pokemon on your team.
  4. The AI will make its move based on the minimax algorithm, aiming to defeat your Pokemon.
  5. The battle continues until one player's entire team is defeated, determining the winner.
